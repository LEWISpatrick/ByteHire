How It Works Features FAQ

How do I get started with ByteHire?

-
Simply create an account, complete your profile, and you can start hiring developers or offering your services immediately. No verification process or approval waiting period.

Is Bitcoin the only payment method?

-
Currently, we only support Bitcoin to ensure maximum privacy, security, and global accessibility. We may add other cryptocurrencies in the future based on community demand.

How does ByteHire ensure quality work?

-
ByteHire has a robust rating and review system. Clients can view developer portfolios, previous work, and ratings before hiring. We also offer an escrow system for milestone-based payments.

What if there's a dispute with a developer?

-
ByteHire offers a dispute resolution system where a neutral third party can review the work and determine a fair outcome. Our escrow system helps protect both parties during the project.
